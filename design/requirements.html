Requirements


  1. R1. Receiving barcode photograph shall detect the identity of person.
  2. R2. Terminal device shall receive person photographs of the iris.
  3. R3. Terminal device shall display the camera image in real time.
  4. R4. Terminal device should have guide mark for the iris on the screen while photographing.
  5. R5. Terminal device shall get age of the person.
  6. R6. Terminal device shall send the data to the main device.
  7. R7. Multiple terminal devices should connect to the main device.
  8. R8. Main device shall send the data come from terminal devices to these devices.
  9. R9. Result of the process shall be displayed on to the device terminals.
